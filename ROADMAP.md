# NBCT Study Tool — Roadmap

## Priority

### 1. Answer Accuracy Audit
- Review every question in questions.json for factual correctness
- Cross-reference against official NBCT standards and authoritative sources
- This is a study tool for a real certification exam — wrong answers actively harm preparation

### 2. Improve MC Distractor Quality
- Wrong answers are currently too easy to eliminate (differ in format, specificity, tone)
- All four MC options should match in structure, length, and level of detail
- Distractors should be plausible — things a teacher might reasonably believe but that don't reflect "accomplished practice"

### 3. Research Official/Authoritative Resources
- Look into official NBCT prep materials, sample questions, scoring rubrics
- Find what other high-quality study resources exist for MC Generalist Component 1
- Incorporate anything useful into the question bank or as reference material

### 4. Weakness-Focused Study Mode
- Track right/wrong per question (already stored in localStorage)
- Verify tracking logic is correct and reliable
- Add a new study mode option: "Focus on Weaknesses" that pulls only from questions the user has gotten wrong or struggled with
- Could also surface domains with low accuracy as suggested focus areas
